Overview

Purposes

The Cache Valley Chamber of Commerce is here to help protect the jobs and interests of the people who work hard to earn a livelihood. Actively sponsoring and participating in job fairs and community service projects and valley-wide picnics for veterans and their families. The chamber's main purpose is to stimulate economic growth with new jobs while using sponsor donations to help the local leaders bring about a healthy work community.

Scenarios

Who, from my community, is already sponsoring this chamber and what are the benefits for joining myself?

What investments will the chamber make with my financial contributions, and do I have a say in how it is used?

How will I stay up to date on any activities or events that the chamber is involved in?
